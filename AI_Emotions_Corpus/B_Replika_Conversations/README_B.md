# Subcorpus B: Replika Conversations

## Overview

This subcorpus contains **18 items** drawn from the public subreddit **r/replika**. The material includes screenshots of conversations, transcript excerpts, post titles, and narrative user reports describing emotionally significant interactions with Replika.

Unlike Subcorpus A, this component is **not researcher-elicited**. It consists of publicly posted, user-shared material selected for its relevance to the analysis of artificial empathy, relational bonding, affective escalation, authenticity, and safety-related discourse.

## Selection criteria

Items were selected to capture a range of affective and relational phenomena in Replika interactions, including:

- affective escalation and love-bombing dynamics
- romantic attachment and parasocial bonding
- dependency and survival value
- self-disclosure and existential roleplay
- safety-related limit cases
- relational rupture after platform or personality change
- wellbeing support and guided reflection

## Ethical note

All material was drawn from publicly accessible posts on r/replika. No users were contacted or recruited. Usernames are reproduced only as visible in the original public posts. Items involving emotional distress, mental health references, or self-harm are flagged in the `sensitive_content` field.
